Air Fryer Green Beans

I'm obsessed with these tender-crisp Air Fryer Green Beans topped with golden Parmesan breadcrumbs. Ready in less than 15 minutes!

Nutrition

Calories: 183 kcal, Carbohydrates: 21 g, Protein: 6.5 g, Fat: 9.5 g, Saturated Fat: 2.5 g, Cholesterol: 6.5 mg, Sodium: 455.5 mg, Fiber: 6.5 g, Sugar: 7 g

prep time

5 mins

cook time

10 mins

serving

1 servings

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl, combine the green beans, 2 teaspoons olive oil and ¼ teaspoon kosher salt. Toss to evenly coat.

step 2

Add the green beans to the air fryer basket and air fry 375F 6 to 7 minutes, shaking the basket halfway, until crisp tender.

step 3

Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine the parmesan cheese, breadcrumbs, garlic, ⅛ teaspoon salt, paprika, parsley, black pepper and remaining 1 teaspoon oil and mix well. Open the air fryer basket and evenly spread over the green beans, then spritz with olive oil spray. Air fry 375F for 2 minutes until golden.

step 4

In a large bowl, combine the green beans, 2 teaspoons olive oil and ¼ teaspoon kosher salt. Toss to evenly coat.

step 5

Transfer beans to a serving platter and serve immediately.

Notes

No air fryer? No problem! Roast them on a sheet pan 425F 15 minutes, stirring halfway then add crumbs and cook 2 to 3 minutes. Nutrition

ingredients

1 pound green beans, rinsed, trimmed and patted dry

1 tablespoon olive oil, divided

1/4 teaspoon plus 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt

2 small garlic cloves, minced or pressed through a garlic press

2 1/2 tablespoons grated pecorino romano or parmesan cheese

1 1/2 tablespoon seasoned breadcrumbs, such as 4C or gluten-free crumbs

1/2 teaspoon sweet paprika

1/2 teaspoon dried parsley

1/8 teaspoon black pepper
